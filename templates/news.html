📢 Government Schemes for Farmers (India)

  • PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi Yojana

    Provides ₹6000 per year financial support to eligible farmers in three installments directly to their bank account.

  • Ministry of Agriculture – Scheme List

    Comprehensive list of central government schemes for crop insurance, irrigation, soil health, farmer welfare and more.

  • Government of India – All Schemes Portal

    Explore all government welfare schemes across departments. Filter by agriculture, education, employment, etc.

  • Agriculture Infrastructure Fund (AIF)

    Offers medium-long term loans with interest subsidy to FPOs, agri startups, and co-ops for warehouses, cold storage, etc.

  • eNAM (National Agriculture Market)

    Online trading platform to connect existing APMC mandis to create a unified national market for agricultural commodities.

  • Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK)

    Find district-level agri experts, on-field trainings, and demonstration units for modern farming practices.
